# SpellBloc Learning Curriculum (Ages 2-7)

## Stage 1: Letter Recognition (Ages 2-3)
**Goal**: Recognize and identify individual letters
- Single vowels: a, e, i, o, u
- Simple consonants: b, c, d, m, p, t
- Letter sounds practice
- Visual letter matching

## Stage 2: Phonics Basics (Ages 3-4)
**Goal**: Learn letter sounds and simple phonics
- Short vowel sounds (CVC pattern)
- 2-letter words: at, it, in, on, up, am
- 3-letter words: cat, dog, bat, sun, mom, dad
- Consonant-vowel combinations

## Stage 3: Simple Words (Ages 4-5)
**Goal**: Spell basic 3-4 letter words
- CVC words: cat, dog, pig, hat, run, sit
- CVCC words: jump, milk, help, fast
- Common sight words: the, and, see, can
- Word families (-at, -an, -et, -in)

## Stage 4: Intermediate Words (Ages 5-6)
**Goal**: Build vocabulary with 4-5 letter words
- Blends: stop, play, tree, frog
- Digraphs: ship, chat, when, this
- Silent e: cake, bike, home, cute
- Common words: water, happy, green

## Stage 5: Advanced Words (Ages 6-7)
**Goal**: Master complex spelling patterns
- Long vowel patterns
- Double consonants: ball, bell, miss
- Compound words: rainbow, cupcake
- 5-6 letter words: flower, school, friend

## Stage 6: Reading Readiness (Ages 7+)
**Goal**: Prepare for reading fluency
- Multi-syllable words
- Common prefixes/suffixes
- Irregular spellings
- Sentence building basics
